# NACC UDS Data Dictionary - Feature Selection

## Non-Medical Features Selected

### Demographic Features
- **SEX**: Subject's sex (self-reported)
- **NACCAGE**: Age at visit (calculated)
- **EDUC**: Years of education
- **RACE**: Racial background
- **MARISTAT**: Marital status
- **HISPANIC**: Hispanic ethnicity

### Social & Lifestyle Features
- **LIVSITUA**: Living situation
- **RESIDENC**: Type of residence
- **INDEPEND**: Independence in daily activities
- **PRIMLANG**: Primary language
- **SMOKYRS**: Years smoked
- **PACKS_PER**: Packs per day

### Known Health Conditions
- **HYPERT**: Hypertension (patient-known)
- **DEP**: Depression (patient-known)
- **STROKE**: Stroke history (patient-known)
- **DIABET**: Diabetes (patient-known)
