# Medical vs Non-Medical Feature Classification

## ✅ NON-MEDICAL (Allowed)
**Justification**: Information that normal people know about themselves without medical tests.

### Demographic (Allowed)
- Age, Gender, Education, Race, Marital Status
- **Reason**: Basic personal information

### Lifestyle (Allowed)
- Smoking history, Alcohol use, Living situation
- **Reason**: Self-reported habits and living conditions

### Known Conditions (Allowed with caution)
- Hypertension, Diabetes, Depression, Stroke history
- **Reason**: Conditions typically known to patients through diagnosis

## ❌ MEDICAL (Not Allowed)
**Justification**: Requires medical expertise, tests, or clinical interpretation.

### Cognitive Tests
- MMSE, MoCA, CDR scores
- **Reason**: Administered by healthcare professionals

### Clinical Measurements
- Blood pressure, Heart rate, Lab results
- **Reason**: Requires medical equipment and interpretation

### Specialist Assessments
- Neurological exams, Psychiatric evaluations
- **Reason**: Requires clinical expertise
